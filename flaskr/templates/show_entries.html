Welcome!

Hello! Thank you for participating in our project, Teaching Information Visualization Yourself (TIVY). You will learn about Matrix Visualization, a powerful visualization format that represents relationships in a network. You will go through a number of tutorials to familiarize yourself with:

  • How to read the matrix visualization of a network.
  • How to find clusters in a network from its matrix visualization
  • How to calculate the density of a network from its matrix visualization.
  • How to calculate degree centrality of a node in the network from its matrix visualization.

Once you are finished with the tutorials, you will be asked a series of test questions to check how well you have understood the tutorials.
